Kaz

Kaz is our Golden Retriever who just turned one year old this winter. I named him after my favorite character from the Grishaverse.

Kaz loves going to the beach and taking baths. He and Inej love playing together and he loves car rides.

He earned his akc puppy S.T.A.R title earlier last year.

Inej

Inej is our Brittany who is almost three years old. I named her after one of my favorite characters in Six of Crows.

Inej likes to swim on the carpet and loves watching birds and squirrels from the window. Inej loves car rides the most.

She has earned her tki and cgca titles and loves barnhunt!
